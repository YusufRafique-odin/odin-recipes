MUG CAKE

Hail, the king of midnight sweet cravings. MUG CAKE . This dish is enjoyed globally
because of its simplicity. I guarantee you will enjoy this mug cake if you follow the recipe well.
Make sure not to eat this too many times as it is very high in sugar and fat.
ENJOY !

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

STEPS:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a large microwave-able mug
  3. Stir dry ingredients well. Make sure there are no lumps
  4. Add in all liquid ingredients
  5. Stir well until finely smooth batter not too runny and not too thick
  6. Cook in the microwave until cake is done in the middle about 1 minute 45 seconds
  7. Enjoy mug cake
